Yamamelodies by Arya Djenar

This interactive map is inspired by my fascination with the unique and exciting jingles played at each station along Tokyo’s Yamanote Line. It’s designed to showcase these distinctive sounds, encourage exploration, and serve as a creative design practice.

Tap on each station’s circle to hear its unique jingle
